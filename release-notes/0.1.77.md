## 0.1.77

Your footer now follows your site theme — plus one-click ready-made styles.

Change your colours under Settings → Theme and your footer restyles along with
the rest of the site, including the newsletter sign-up. Light footer themes are
now fully legible too — the sign-up field and button adapt to the footer instead
of staying dark.

This release also adds eight ready-made styles to Settings → Theme — Obsidian &
Gold, Studio Mono, Sunrise Warm, Forest Trade, Parchment, Carbon, Sand & Sea, and
Ink — so you can restyle your whole site in one click, then fine-tune any colour.
The header and footer "theme" pickers are now labelled "tone (light or dark)" to
make clear they only set light-or-dark, while your colours come from Settings →
Theme.

Updating applies everything automatically.
