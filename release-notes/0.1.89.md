## 0.1.89

Your site now comes with a full **SEO suite** — titles, social previews, sitemaps, and one-click search-engine setup — plus a calmer editing canvas.

### New — a complete SEO suite, built in

A new **SEO** area in the dashboard gives your site everything it needs to be found, with no plugin and no developer:

- **Titles & Meta.** Decide how each kind of page — your home page, blog posts, projects, and the rest — appears in search results, with templates so every new page is covered automatically.
- **Social & Schema.** Control the preview that shows when your pages are shared (the image, title, and description), add your social handles, and ship structured data so search engines understand your content.
- **Sitemaps & Crawl.** A sitemap is generated and kept current for you, so crawlers can discover every published page — and you decide what they're allowed to crawl.
- **Connect & Verify.** Prove you own the site to Google and other engines in a couple of clicks, right from the dashboard.
- **Instant indexing.** New and changed pages can ping Bing, Yandex, and more the moment they publish, and you can connect a Google key to push fresh pages straight to Google too.

You can also fine-tune a page's title and social preview **right in the editor** while you work — no jumping between screens.

### New — a calmer editing canvas

- **Notifications you can actually see.** Save, publish, and draft messages now appear neatly in the bottom-right, above the "Stop editing" button, instead of slipping behind the top bar.
- **The assistant moved to the bottom-left**, so it no longer sits on top of your save messages.
- **The outline no longer pops open** every time you enter edit mode — open it from the admin bar when you want it, and your choice sticks.

### Also fixed

- **Turning AI off now saves cleanly.** Disabling AI and clearing the fields — including a new **Clear saved key** button that removes your stored key — now saves without a hitch.

Updating applies everything automatically.
