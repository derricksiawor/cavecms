## 0.1.59

Make CaveCMS your own. A new **Theme** page lets you set your brand colours —
and your logo size — from the dashboard, with a live preview and a built-in
readability check. No code or CSS required.

### Settings → Theme: your brand colours, from the dashboard

Pick your **Primary**, **Secondary**, and **Accent** colours plus the light
and dark surface shades, choose a light or dark base, and watch the whole
public site re-skin in a live preview before you save. Headings, buttons,
links, and accents pick up your brand colours automatically across every page.

A built-in **contrast guardrail** (WCAG + APCA) warns you when a colour pairing
would be hard to read — so you can brand boldly without accidentally making
text disappear into its background.

Your dashboard keeps its own neutral, legible theme no matter what you choose
for the public site, so a bold or low-contrast public palette can never make
the admin itself hard to use.

### Configurable logo height

The header and footer logo heights are now adjustable from Settings, so your
logo sits at the size that suits its proportions instead of a fixed default.
