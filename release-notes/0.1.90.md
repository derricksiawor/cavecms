## 0.1.90

Two fixes on top of 0.1.89.

### Fixed — turning AI off always saves

Unticking **Enable AI features** now switches off every AI surface along with it. Before, the surface toggles stayed on behind the scenes, so simply turning AI off could fail to save with a vague "some fields look off." Disabling AI — and clearing your saved key — now saves cleanly, every time.

### Fixed — release notes read properly

The **What's new** notes on the Updates screen and the dashboard's update card now render as formatted text — headings, emphasis, and lists — instead of showing raw markdown.

Updating applies everything automatically.
