## 0.1.75

Completes laptop / self-managed in-app updates.

Installs that run CaveCMS as a plain foreground process (a laptop or dev box,
with no systemd unit or PM2 to manage them) could get stuck or roll back when
updating from the dashboard. Two paths the updater needs — where it writes its
logs and where it reads your install's settings — were defaulting to
system locations that only exist on a root-managed server. They now point at
your install's own directory, so the dashboard updater installs the new
version cleanly and then asks you to restart your CaveCMS process to finish.
Hosted installs (systemd, PM2, cPanel) are unchanged.

Updating applies everything automatically.
