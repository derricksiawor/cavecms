## 0.1.30

Publisher-side release-history cleanup + in-app updater verification
target.

### Changed

**Publisher prunes old releases automatically.** Previously every
release on `cavecms.derricksiawor.com/releases/` accumulated forever
— 27 versions on disk at last count, ~864 MB of zips plus sidecars.
`scripts/release/publish.mjs` (publisher-only, gitignored) now sorts
the on-disk zips by semver-descending and `rm -f`s anything past the
10 most recent (zip + sha256 + sig). Manifest entries are NOT pruned
— they're tiny JSON and the in-app "Recent updates" history surface
benefits from the longer tail.

Also serves as the upgrade target for the 0.1.29 install's in-app
updater verification — no other functional changes.

### Migration impact

None. Publisher-side cleanup runs on the publisher box during
`pnpm release:publish`. Customers see no change.
