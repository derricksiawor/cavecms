## 0.1.70

A small fix for the admin dashboard.

When your site sits behind Cloudflare's email-protection feature, contact and
account email addresses in the dashboard (the top bar, the Leads, Subscribers,
Users, and Activity tables, and the on-site edit bar) could render incorrectly
and log harmless console errors. Those email addresses now display cleanly.
No changes to your public site.

Updating applies everything automatically.
