## 0.2.7

Fixes the dashboard updater and restore health check on cPanel / LiteSpeed hosting.

### Fixed — updates and restores complete on cPanel / LiteSpeed

The health check that confirms your site is up (after an update or a restore) was pinned to your server's internal loopback address. On cPanel/LiteSpeed that pin doesn't reach your site — the web server answers it with a default 404 instead of your site — so the check failed and the update stopped at "Getting ready" (and a restore could stall at "Verifying"). The check now uses your site's real public address, which routes correctly, so updates and restores finish normally. Your site's own security is unchanged: the check still only ever talks to your own domain.
