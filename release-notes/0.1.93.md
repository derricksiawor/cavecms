## 0.1.93

A small but welcome polish to the Updates screen: when a new version is waiting, it now sits right at the top where you can act on it.

### Improved — the available update is front and center

On **Settings → Updates**, a ready-to-install version now appears as the very first card on the page — above your current version and its release notes — so the one button you came to click is the first thing you see. When you're already up to date, nothing changes. Nothing to configure; it just works the next time an update lands.
