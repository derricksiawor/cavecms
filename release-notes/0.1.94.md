## 0.1.94

The Updates screen now leads with your status, so the first thing you see is where you stand.

### Improved — your update status sits right at the top

On **Settings → Updates**, the status card — whether that's a ready-to-install **update** or a reassuring **"You're up to date"** — now appears as the very first card on the page, above your running version and its release notes. When an update lands, the install button is the first thing in front of you; when you're current, you get a clean all-clear at a glance. Nothing to set up.
