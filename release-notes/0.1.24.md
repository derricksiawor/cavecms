## 0.1.24

Third in-app updater hotfix on CLI-installed sites.

### Fixed

**Update apply stalled at "Preparing your data" after launching the
detached orchestrator.** 0.1.16 + 0.1.18 both fixed earlier blockers
(status-path 500 + missing orchestrator helpers), but a third bug
remained: the apply route's env-forward list doesn't include
`CAVECMS_STATE_DIR`. When the apply route spawns the orchestrator
in a detached process, the orchestrator inherits a small allowlist
of env vars — and `CAVECMS_STATE_DIR` (the per-install state dir
the runtime user owns) wasn't in it. The orchestrator's path
resolver fell back to the system default `/var/lib/cavecms/`, hit
EPERM on the lock + status writes (no `cavecmsstate` group at
process-start time), and stalled the modal at step 1.

Fixed in two places:

1. `app/api/admin/updates/apply/route.ts` — added `CAVECMS_STATE_DIR`
   to the env-forward allowlist so the detached orchestrator sees
   the same per-install state path the route itself wrote into the
   status file.
2. `scripts/lib/cavecms-update-helpers.sh` — same addition to the
   `--setenv=` forward list when the orchestrator subsequently
   spawns the watchdog via `systemd-run --user --scope`.

### Migration impact

Customers re-installing from 0.1.24+ get the fixed orchestrator
bundled in their standalone tree. Customers stuck on 0.1.16-0.1.23
installs that still hit the "Preparing your data" stall need a
fresh re-install — the orchestrator can't self-update past its
own broken env-forward step.
