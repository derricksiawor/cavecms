## 0.1.73

Better updates for local and self-managed installs, plus a small dashboard fix.

If you run CaveCMS as a plain foreground process (a laptop or dev box, not
under a service manager), the in-app updater now finishes cleanly: it installs
and builds the new version, then asks you to restart your CaveCMS process to
load it — instead of trying to restart a process it can't manage and rolling
the update back. Hosted installs (systemd, PM2, cPanel) keep updating fully
automatically, exactly as before.

Also fixed: a harmless console error in the page editor's "Recent activity"
list. No changes to your public site. Updating applies everything
automatically.
