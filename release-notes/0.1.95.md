## 0.1.95

A major reliability pass on the page editor — undo always works, your edits never get lost, and the whole surface feels smoother — plus a fix that keeps your contact forms from ever dropping a lead.

### Improved — undo, redo, and never losing your work

Undo and redo are now rock-solid across the editor. Deleting a block, reordering, or any edit can be reliably undone — and redone — whether you use the **Undo** button, the keyboard (⌘Z / ⇧⌘Z), or the little "Undo" that appears right after an action. They all stay in lockstep, with no lag before the buttons light up.

Your work is safer, too. Image descriptions and other field edits now save even if you close the panel a moment too quickly, and a half-finished block no longer interrupts you with an error — it simply waits for you to fill in what's missing.

### Improved — a smoother editing surface

Editing feels more responsive everywhere. Inserting a block keeps its spinner until the block actually appears (no more "did that work?" flicker), the empty-page prompt shows the instant you remove the last block, and the publish toolbar appears the moment you start editing a page inline. The colour and gradient pickers now use the same elegant in-app picker as the rest of the studio, and the link tool in rich-text fields is inline — no browser pop-up.

### Fixed — your contact forms never drop a lead

If Google's spam check is briefly slow or unreachable, your contact, enquiry, and brochure forms now let the submission through (and flag it for review) instead of silently discarding it. Real enquiries always reach your inbox.

### Fixed — a few smaller things

Your site logo, browser-tab icon, and social-share image are now protected from accidental deletion in the media library, and a handful of editor and rendering rough edges have been smoothed over.
