## 0.1.96

Forms can now do far more than collect a message. Build the form you want, hand visitors a file the moment they submit, pull dropdown choices straight from your own content, and route every lead to your CRM — all from the form builder, no code.

### New — deliver a file when someone submits

Turn any form into a gated download. When a visitor fills it in, they get your PDF — a brochure, price list, lookbook, anything — either emailed to them or offered instantly on the page. Haven't set up email yet? The download simply appears on-screen, so the file is never lost. Each link is private and expires after a week.

### New — dropdowns that build themselves from your content

A form dropdown can pull its choices live from your tags or categories. Add a new category and it appears in the form automatically — no retyping your options in two places.

### New — hidden fields to tag your leads

Quietly attach a fixed value to every submission — a campaign code, a source tag, a project id — without showing it to the visitor. It travels with the lead and into your CRM, so you always know where an enquiry came from.

### New — send any form's leads to your CRM

CRM mapping for HubSpot and Zoho used to be tied to the built-in contact form. Now you can point any form you build at your CRM and map its fields — per form, or as a site-wide default.

### Improved — a clearer view of every submission

Open a lead and its submission is laid out field by field — every question and answer in a tidy list — instead of one run-together block of text.
