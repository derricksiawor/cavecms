## 0.1.88

Cloud backups are ready to connect.

### Now available — Google Drive & OneDrive

The off-site backup destinations are live. Under **Settings → Backups**, **Google Drive** and **OneDrive** now show a **Connect** button: link your own account in a few seconds — pick a provider and approve the short code on Google or Microsoft's own page — and CaveCMS can keep an encrypted, automatic, off-site copy of your whole site, with one-click restore. It's included; there's nothing to buy.

Updating applies everything automatically.
