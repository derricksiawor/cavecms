## 0.1.64

Fixes automatic rollback falsely reporting failure.

When you rolled a site back to its previous version — from the dashboard, the
`cavecms rollback` command, or automatically after a problem update — the
rollback would restore your files, database, and settings correctly, but the
recovery would still be recorded as "failed" and tell you to contact support.
The site was actually fine; only the status was wrong.

Rollback now reports success when it succeeds, and the running version it
reports matches the version that was restored.

Updating applies everything automatically.
